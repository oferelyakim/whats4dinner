### Section: Replanish AI

**Eyebrow:** Optional. Powerful. $6/month.

**Headline:** AI that helps you decide tonight, not plan your year.

**Body:**
Most meal-planning apps lean on AI to generate your whole week. We don't. The weekly drop is curated, free, and works without AI.

Replanish AI is the *swap* — the moment when you've got a plan but tonight isn't quite right. Tap a meal, say "make it vegan" or "I have chicken and broccoli, what can I make?" and it rewrites just that slot.

**4 features:**

- **Per-meal swap & personalize** — "make this dinner vegan", "swap chicken for tofu", "lower carbs"
- **Pantry / leftover reroll** — "I have chicken and broccoli" → 3 dishes that match
- **Unlimited recipe imports** — save any recipe URL to your bank (free is capped at 10/month)
- **Smart shopping consolidation** — dedupe across the week, suggest bulk buys, build the cheapest cart

**Pricing block:**
- $6 / month
- $60 / year (14-day free trial)
- 4 seats — share with your household

**CTA:** Start your free trial

### Section: Trust

**Eyebrow:** Where the recipes come from.

**Body:**
Replanish doesn't generate recipes. We surface them — link-first — from food blogs and recipe sites whose authors put real work into them. We store the ingredient list (which is factual data) and link out for the full instructions, so the original source gets the click and the credit.

Every domain we pull from is listed at [`/sources`](/sources). If you're a content owner and you want your site removed from our bank, [contact us](/contact) and we'll take it down.
