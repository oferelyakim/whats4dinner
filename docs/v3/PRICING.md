## FAQ (billing-specific)

### How does the 14-day trial work?
The trial is on the annual plan ($60/year). You add a payment method, get 14 days of full Replanish AI access, and we charge $60 on day 15 unless you cancel. Cancel any time during the trial — no charge.

### Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Monthly cancels at the end of the current billing period. Annual cancels at renewal — you keep access through the year you paid for.

### What happens if I cancel?
You drop to the Free tier on the next billing date. Your circles, recipes, plans, lists, events — all stay yours. The four AI features (swap, reroll, consolidation, event planner) and unlimited URL imports go away.

### How does the 4-seat sharing work?
The owner of the subscription can invite up to 3 other people from their household by email or by adding them from a shared circle. Each seat gets full AI access while the subscription is active. There's no per-seat charge.

### Is there a family / yearly bundle?
The annual plan ($60/yr) **is** the family bundle — 4 seats included at no extra charge. We don't sell per-seat plans separately.

### Do you take refunds?
Yes. Email us within 7 days of purchase and we refund in full. After 7 days, we cancel future renewals but don't refund the current period.

### Why is the core app free?
Two reasons. First, meal planning shouldn't be paywalled — every household deals with it. Second, our long-term revenue is shopping cart partnerships (Walmart-first, Instacart and Amazon Fresh planned), and depth-of-corpus + active users are what makes that pitch work.

### Do you sell my data?
No. We never sell user data. We share aggregated, anonymous metrics (e.g., "X recipes are trending this week") with retailer partners. Personal account data — recipes, lists, plans, contacts — never leaves your circle without your action.
