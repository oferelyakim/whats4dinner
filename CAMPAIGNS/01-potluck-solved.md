## The Viral Mechanic Explained

**The flow that creates organic growth:**

1. User creates a potluck event in Replanish (5 minutes)
2. User taps "Share with guests" → copies link → pastes into WhatsApp group
3. 8 guests open the link on their phones
4. They see: event name, host name, the menu, who's claimed what, and what's still open
5. They tap "I'll bring the salad" — prompted for name + email
6. They think: "This is better than the group chat. How did she do this?"
7. After claiming, they see: "Want to organize your own? Free, 60 seconds → replanish.app"
8. 1–2 of them sign up. Each creates events of their own.

**K-factor estimate (conservative):** Each event link reaches 8 guests. 15% convert = 1.2 new users per event. If each new user creates one event, K = 1.2 (viral by definition).
