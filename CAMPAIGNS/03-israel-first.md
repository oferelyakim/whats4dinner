## Cultural Calendar Angles

Israeli family life runs on a weekly and annual rhythm distinct from American/European markets. Map Replanish content to these moments:

### Weekly Rhythm
| Moment | Content Angle | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| **Sunday** — start of the Israeli work week | "כבר תכננתם את השבוע?" (Did you plan your week yet?) | Instagram Stories, TikTok |
| **Wednesday** — midweek shopping trigger | "מה צריך לקנות לפני שישי?" (What do we need to buy before Friday?) | Instagram, WhatsApp status |
| **Thursday night** | "מה מביאים לארוחה של שישי?" (Who's bringing what to Friday dinner?) | TikTok, Instagram Stories |
| **Friday morning** | "ערב שישי מאורגן" (Organized Friday evening) | Instagram Stories poll, WhatsApp |

### Annual Calendar (2026 Key Moments)

| Date | Event | Content Angle |
|---|---|---|
| **Sep 22, 2026** | Rosh Hashana | Seder coordination — who brings what to the holiday table |
| **Oct 1, 2026** | Yom Kippur | Pre-fast meal planning, break-fast coordination |
| **Oct 6–13, 2026** | Sukkot | Sukkah meals — multi-family coordination use case |
| **Oct 13, 2026** | Simchat Torah | End of holiday cycle — back to routine content |
| **Dec 2026** | Hanukkah | Sufganiyot party planning, family gatherings |
| **Mar/Apr 2027** | Passover Seder | The BIGGEST use case — 20-person seder coordination |

**Note:** Passover is Replanish's single strongest event coordination use case. The Seder is an event with: a menu (matzah ball soup, brisket, etc.), assigned dishes (every family member brings something), supplies (haggadahs, afikoman bag), and tasks (who sets the table, who leads). This is literally what the Events feature was built for. **Time a major launch push for Passover 2027.**

## WhatsApp Strategy

### Why WhatsApp Is the #1 Israeli Acquisition Channel

WhatsApp penetration in Israel is 99%. It is how Israelis communicate — more than email, more than SMS. Every Israeli family has multiple WhatsApp groups: the family group, the school parents' group, the neighborhood group, the friends' group.

A recommendation in a WhatsApp family group reaches 20–100 people who all personally know and trust the sender. This beats any ad.

### How to Make Replanish Spread Through WhatsApp

**Mechanic 1: The Potluck Link**

Every time a user creates an event and shares the invite link, that link goes into a WhatsApp group. This is the organic WhatsApp distribution. The event invite page needs to:
- Open beautifully on mobile (no horizontal scroll, full RTL)
- Show a clear "Powered by Replanish" footer with link
- After claiming a dish, show: "תכנן את האירוע הבא שלך — replanish.app" (Plan your next event — replanish.app)

Pre-populated WhatsApp share text (include this in the "Share event" flow):
```
ארוחת [שם האירוע] — [תאריך] 🍽️
לראות מה מביא מה וסימון: [לינק]
(נוצר ב-Replanish)
```

## Notes and Reminders

- **Deploy the app with replanish.app domain before any press outreach.** The Vercel URL (`whats4dinner-gamma.vercel.app`) is a trust-killer in a press pitch.
- **The Hebrew content should ALWAYS feel native, not translated.** Have a Hebrew native speaker review all content before publishing. Machine-translated Hebrew is instantly obvious to Israelis.
- **Israeli humor wins.** Overly polished American-style content performs worse. Be real, be direct, be a little self-deprecating. "אפליקציה שבניתי כי נמאס לי מהכאוס" (An app I built because I was sick of the chaos) is a better hook than a polished brand statement.
- **Shabbat timing matters.** Never post new content between Friday sunset and Saturday night. Schedule posts for Sunday morning (start of Israeli work week) for maximum reach.
- **Passover 2027 is the target.** Start building the audience now so you have a real base when the Seder planning content season arrives. The event coordination feature will demonstrate its full value in that moment.
